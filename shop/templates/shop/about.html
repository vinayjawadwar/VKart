About Us

Vkart Private Limited, Vkart is an Indian e-commerce company based in Bengaluru, India. It was founded by Vinay Jawadwar and Kapil Mathpati in 2020. The company initially focused on Handicraft sales, before expanding into other product categories such as Art, fashion, wooden works and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal. As of March 2020, Vkart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. Vkart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones. Vkart also owns Paytm, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).

History

VKart was founded in january 2020 by Vinay Jawadwar and Kapil Mathpati, who were both alumni of the Mgm's College Of Engineering,Nanded . The company initially focused on online book sales with country-wide shipping. Following its launch, VKart slowly grew in prominence; by 2020, it was receiving 10000 orders per day. In 2020, VKart acquired the Bangalore-based social book discovery service weRead from Lulu.com.

Business structure

According to a report in November 2020, VKart was operating through a complex business structure which included nine firms, some registered in Singapore and some in India. Vkarts co-founders sold WS Retail to a consortium of investors led by Rajeev Kuchhal.
